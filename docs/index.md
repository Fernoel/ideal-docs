## General Introduction

The Ideal Sale platform, developed by Design House, is a tool for managing digital content, such as promotional circulars. It includes various modules for creating, organizing, and publishing content related to brands, stores, and promotions.

This manual focuses on the **Circular Builder** module, which allows users to create and manage individual content items or groups of content for digital circulars.
