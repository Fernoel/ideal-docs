# Card Styles

Define the visual appearance of product cards.

## Customization Options
- **Border**: Color, width, radius.
- **Shadow**: Drop shadow intensity.
- **Typography**: Font family, size, and weight for price and description.
- **Price Layout**: Position of the dollar sign, cents, and unit.
- **Background**: Color or pattern.

## Usage
Apply different styles to highlight specific deals (e.g., "Hot Buy" style vs "Standard" style).
